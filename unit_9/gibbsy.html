Client: New Balance, Nicole Gibbs

Role: Web Designer

Date: May '15-present

Type: Design Ethos, Web Design, Responsive Design, Branding

Skills: InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop

More: On the rise professional tennis player Nicole Gibbs, represented by New Balance, reached out to me to design her website. New Balance is in the process of creating websites for all of their athletes in hopes of raising their awareness and fan bases. I started by creating a mood board, selecting fonts and colors, and making a site map, and then moved into making a responsive website that heavily linked to Nicole's social media pages. This project is still in the works and should be live by late March or April.
