Client: Media & Society Group Project

Role: Layout Designer

Date: December '14

Type: Print Layout, Booklet Creation

Skills: InDesign

More: In 2014, I created a 32-page zine with the help of my group for a Media & Society class at Tufts. Entitled Instagram in Print, our zine explored the effects of Instagram and viewing it in print rather than digitally on a screen. We all contributed our own content, and I was the one who laid out all of the spreads in InDesign.
