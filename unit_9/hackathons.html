Client: Tufts Hackathon

Role: Event Designer

Date: September '14-April '15

Type: Logo Design, T-Shirt Design, Facebook Optimization

Skills: Illustrator, Photoshop

More: I created the logo for Tufts' Polyhack hackathon event. The goal of the event was to get students of all academic backgrounds involved, so I knew that I wanted to go in a direction that incorporated three different colors to represent the different disciplines. Polyhack was so successful in the fall that Tufts partnered with Harvard and MIT in the spring to put on IDHack (International Development Hack). For this hackathon, I was inspired by the global scope of the event, so I used a digitized map of the world paired with an edgy wordmark.
