More: In my final semester at Tufts, I was assigned a group project to explain the process of evolution to a middle school audience. My group decided that an infographic would work best for 10-14-year-olds because of its engaging diagrams and easy-to-read blurbs. I challenged myself with the monochromatic color scheme and compiled each of my group members' sections of text into the infographic you see to the left.
