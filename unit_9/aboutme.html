Hi, My Name is...

Bella Oriella! And yes, my name really does rhyme. I've been a graphic designer for the past seven years and have a wide variety of experience with Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign. I dabble in everything from print to digital, layout to logos, and apparel to branding. I freelance regularly and am currently looking for a job as a creative at an advertising agency, a designer at a well-known company, or a layout designer at a magazine.

I graduated from Tufts University in 2015 with a major in Art History and a minor in Communications. While I'm originally from Cleveland, I'm looking forward to living in a bigger city like Boston, Chicago, New York, or D.C. and learning more about the art of design.
