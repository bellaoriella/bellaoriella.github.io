Client: Harvard Public Health

Role: Freelance Graphic Designer

Date: July-September '15

Type: Rebranding, Booklet Design

Skills: InDesign, Illustrator

More: Harvard Public Health reached out to me to rebrand their annual event, the U.S.-China Health Summit. They were looking for a sleek, professional rebrand that could be used in years to come. Because the event switches between Boston and Beijing every year, I put a silhouette of each city on the front and back covers.
